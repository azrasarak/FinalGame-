HOW TO PLAY THE GAME:
  • Follow the CIRCLES with your mouse
  • After each circle, your score will go up by 100
  • Each level will get more diffult
  • Use the slider at the bottom of the screen, to control the volume of the music (it'll take a few seconds to start)
